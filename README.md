This repository contains a complete collection of laboratory reports from Digital Electronics Circuits courses. The work includes full schematics, simulations, timing analysis, and device characterization using Cadence Virtuoso and SPICE.

Included Labs:
Lab 1 – Linux & SPICE Introduction
Lab 2 – Inverter Amplifier (SPICE)
Lab 3 – Digital Inverter Amplifier
Lab 4 – MOS Transistor Characterization
Lab 5 – CMOS Inverter: Vm extraction & β tuning
Lab 6 – CMOS Logic Gates: NAND/NOR/AND, delays, energy & power


Tools & Methods:
Cadence Virtuoso (schematic design, DC/AC/transient simulations)
SPICE analysis
Linux command-line workflow
Digital timing measurements (tphl, tplh, tpd)
Dynamic energy and static power calculations


Purpose:
This repository demonstrates practical experience in CMOS design, transistor-level behavior, and digital circuit analysis using industry-standard tools.
